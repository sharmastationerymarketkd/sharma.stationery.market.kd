Started as a single counter in 1985, now stocking three floors of everything you'll ever need to write, draw, wrap or file.

Sharma Stationery Market began as a small footpath counter run by Mr. Ramesh Sharma, selling exercise books to students from the neighbouring school. Four decades on, the shop is run by the second generation and stocks everything from geometry boxes to office paper shredders — while keeping the same habit of remembering regular customers by name.

We still believe a stationery shop should feel like a second pencil box: a little cramped, a little chaotic, and exactly where you find what you didn't know you needed.
